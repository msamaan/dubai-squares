About Dubai squares

Welcome to the exciting Dubai Squares brand world.

The Dubai Squares Corporate Identity manual provides direction on how to represent the Dubai Squares brand look and feel across all marketing and operational material.

These guidelines ensure a common language and foundation for all marketing and communication of Dubai Squares, ensuring we create a strong brand identity that captures the experience of every iconic, loveable space within Dubai.

To build a distinctive Dubai Squares brand, it is vital to maintain a consistent identity throughout all our communications and touchpoints.

For any queries regarding the branding, messaging or asset sharing, please email branding@dubaisquares.com

Our vision

Placemaking public squares that are:

1- Artistic “experiential-destinations”
2- Future-worthy
3- Thriving with life within the remarkable Dubai Emirate

Placemaking is the act of making places. It is a process of creation. The concept originated in the 1960s when planners introduced a new approach to the design of public spaces. Placemaking in essence aims to understand a place with all its facets and thereby create a holistic experience.

Our mission

To plan, develop, and manage Dubai’s vibrant public spaces, contributing to Dubai’s quality of life and sustainable living.

1- Expanding green landscapes and environmental elements to support flexible walking and movement / transportation
2- Increasing investment landscape and providing areas for private sector for commercial and leisure purposes (seasonal restaurants, art activities)
3- Adding an artistic dimension to public squares and transforming it to prime destinations/locations in the Emirate
4- Implementing open air art, cultural, and musical exhibitions, workshops, and supportive activities
