Why invest with us

Dubai Squares is a joint partnership between the government and private sector aiming to achieve the following objectives:

Expanding green landscapes and environmental elements to support flexible walking and movement / transportation Increasing investment landscape and providing areas for private sector for commercial and leisure purposes (seasonal restaurants, art activities) Adding an artistic dimension to public squares and transforming it to prime destinations/locations in the Emirate Implementing open air art, cultural, and musical exhibitions, workshops, and supportive activities
